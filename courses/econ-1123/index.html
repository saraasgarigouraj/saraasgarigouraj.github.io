ECON 1123 – Principles of Economics

August 6, 2024 · Sara Asgari

Semester: Fall 2023

Course Description#

An introductory course covering both microeconomic and macroeconomic principles, tailored for undergraduates from various disciplines.

Course Overview#

Through active Canvas engagement, weekly assignments, and concept-based lectures, students gained foundational knowledge in economic reasoning and the functioning of economies at both individual and national levels.
